🔒 Canadian Privacy Framework

Canada's health data privacy landscape is a patchwork of federal and provincial law. Unlike the US (HIPAA) or EU (GDPR), there is no single statute — the applicable law depends on the province of operation, whether data crosses provincial borders, and whether the organization qualifies as a "health information custodian." This project maps its entire pipeline to the strictest applicable standard in each jurisdiction.
